Frequently Asked Questions

Complete transparency regarding our mechanics, security, and wealth management protocols.

Why should I work with a wealth advisor instead of investing directly through apps?

Direct apps give you access to execute trades, but they do not actively manage risk, rebalance during market crashes, or analyze overlapping funds. We act as your fractional CFO—structuring the strategy, handling the panic, booking the profits, and ensuring your exact goals are flawlessly mathematically tracked so you don't have to guess.

Are my funds locked in with you or can I withdraw anytime?

You have 100% absolute sovereign control over your corpus. Unless deployed in mandatory lock-in funds (like ELSS for Tax Saving), your funds are highly liquid. You can withdraw your money entirely to your registered bank account at any time without asking for our permission.

How do you track and manage my portfolio on a daily basis?

Upon onboarding, you receive access to our centralized digital dashboard. The portfolio is tracked continuously against benchmarks using institutional software. We monitor XIRR, expense ratio leaks, and asset allocations, stepping in dynamically when the portfolio drifts from the prescribed strategy.

What are your fees and charges?

Transparency is our core principle. Our advisory and distribution structures are completely compliant with SEBI regulations. Depending on the model you sign up for (Direct Advisory vs Regular Distribution), fees are either structured as a transparent flat AUM charge or integrated directly by the AMCs. We detail the exact numbers before you invest a single rupee.

How secure are my investments with your firm?

We do not hold your money. Your capital is strictly routed from your personal bank account directly to the Asset Management Companies (AMCs) and held securely in Folios physically registered to your PAN and Aadhaar. We only hold the tactical authority to advise, map, and execute on your behalf.

How quickly can I expect responses from your team?

We pride ourselves on VIP execution. For active clients, WhatsApp messages and calls are answered within minutes. Formal email requests (like mandate changes or deep portfolio audits) are resolved within a strictly enforced 2-hour SLA during standard operating hours.
